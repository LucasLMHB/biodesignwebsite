OUR SOLUTION

MAAScreen, our eco-friendly and biodegradable sunscreens, resolves many environmental issues that arise with the conventional sunscreens on the market. The dangerous chemical products in these sunscreens like oxybenzone threatens our marine life through coral bleaching. Our mission is to extract an important natural occurring amino acid, known as mycosporine like amino acid (MAA) from these algae, which can protect us from the harmful UV rays of the sun. What is also unique about our product is that they are tailored and portioned out perfectly for each consumer. No need to worry about the right amount of sunscreen you should apply.

Our approach to production

The main source of our MAA will be from an algae-farm to grow the algae under optimized MAA-producing conditions. Furthermore, we devote a branch of MAAScreen to a sustainable initiative. Around spring, teams of employees will go on site of the most damaging algae blooms and harvest the harmful algae out of the water, to be used by our company. This approach not only helps the ocean by reducing oxybenzone-related damage, but also directly helps the damage that has already been done by cleaning up harmful algae blooms (HABs).
